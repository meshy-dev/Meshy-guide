# What is Mesh Topology in 3D Graphics

Mesh topology in 3D graphics is the network of **vertices, edges, and faces (polygons)** that define the surface of a 3D object.

It is the **fundamental data structure behind every 3D model**, including:
- Games
- VFX (visual effects)
- Product design
- AR / VR

---

## Intuition

Think of mesh topology like a **chicken-wire lattice wrapped around a shape**.

- The **finer the mesh** → the more detail  
- The **cleaner the topology** → the better it animates and deforms  

Good topology is critical for:
- rigging
- animation
- physics simulation
- real-time rendering
